# Ideation: Business Model Enhancer  (`business-model-enhancer`)

## Status
Ran (proposed 3×4).

## Proposed menu
### Set A — Scope extensions
- **A1. "What would change my mind"** — for the recommended pick, state the 1-2 assumptions that, if false, flip it · cost: S · wedge: strengthens
- **A2. Named-framework lens pack** — optional JTBD / Business Model Canvas / PESTLE lenses on divergence/pre-mortem · cost: M · wedge: neutral
- **A3. Competitor/market reality check** — optional web pass on saturation, feeds the pre-mortem · cost: M · wedge: strengthens (adds external dep)
- **A4. Cheapest-validation step** — append the smallest experiment to de-risk the winner before building · cost: S · wedge: strengthens

### Set B — Robustness & safety
- **B1. Anchor-integrity guard** — self-catch + rewrite candidates that drift into generic, un-anchored advice · cost: S · wedge: strengthens
- **B2. Zero-to-one refusal guard** — detect "no business yet", decline+redirect instead of faking an enhancement · cost: S · wedge: strengthens
- **B3. Lens-not-applicable handling** — mark a non-fitting lens N/A with a reason vs forcing a weak candidate · cost: S · wedge: strengthens
- **B4. Intake consistency check** — flag internally inconsistent economics before diverging · cost: S · wedge: neutral

### Set C — Delight & leverage
- **C1. Scored comparison table** — candidates × (upside·effort·risk·fit) table so convergence is visibly earned · cost: S · wedge: strengthens
- **C2. Adjustable risk appetite** — conservative vs bold weighting of convergence scoring · cost: S · wedge: neutral
- **C3. Save decision brief to file** — dated markdown decision record · cost: S · wedge: neutral
- **C4. Devil's-advocate pass on the winner** — one extra independent pre-mortem on the recommended option · cost: S · wedge: strengthens
